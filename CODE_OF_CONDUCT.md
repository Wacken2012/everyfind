# Code of Conduct

We want Everyfind to be an open, welcoming, and respectful community. All contributors and participants are expected to follow this simple code of conduct.

Be respectful
- Treat others with respect and kindness.
- Be open to feedback and constructive criticism.
- Avoid hostile or abusive language.

Be inclusive
- Be welcoming to people of all backgrounds, identities, and levels of experience.
- Avoid jokes or language that may be exclusionary or offensive.

Collaborate constructively
- Focus on solving problems, not assigning blame.
- When giving feedback, be specific and kind.

Reporting
- If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue addressed to maintainers, or email the project owner.

Consequences
- Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, block users, or close issues/PRs that violate this policy.

This project follows common community norms (see Contributor Covenant for a fuller template). By participating you agree to follow this code of conduct.

For the full Contributor Covenant text, see:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct/
